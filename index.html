SUNDAY GIRL

Using Premier Pro my team and I created a Charlie Chaplin insipired dark comedy, Sunday Girl, it is a silent comedy that follows the story of a young girl being chased by a killer. We chose this for various reasons, but mostly because we did not have sound equipment and had to improvise. It also made it easier to deal with lighting during shoots, because everything was edited to be in black and white. This was probably the most fun group project ever and I highly recommend you to watch the Bloopers.
